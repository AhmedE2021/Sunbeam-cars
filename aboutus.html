Our History

The company SunbeamCars Inc. was established more than 30 years ago by the couple Lisa and Peter, who together are responsible for the day-to- day operations. The couple's two daughters and their sons-in-law help out when things are busy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to go beyond the expectations of our clients, always going that extra step ahead always aiming to excellence and uniqueness, key words of our business. Each representative of Classic Cars Team works hard to join forces in one same direction and, with our Vintage Car Rental Company, we aim to provide an unmatched tailor-made service and establish a completely trustful and engaging relationship with our clients. We are committed to the idea that customers expect great value for money: deliver impeccable service and keeping it as convenient as possible is exactly what we do.
